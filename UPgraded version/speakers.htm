Speakers

IMRAN-ALI-DINA

GRAPHICS DESIGNER

Imran Ali Dina, known as GFX Mentor, is a Pakistani graphic designer, educator, and YouTuber from Karachi. With 20+ years of experience, he has trained thousands of students. He founded the GFX Mentor YouTube channel in 2016, offering free tutorials on Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects. His simple teaching style makes learning easy. Before YouTube, he taught at Iqra University and worked on professional design projects. His work has helped many beginners and professionals improve their skills.

IRFAN MALIK

CEO XEVEN SOLUTIONS

Irfan Malik, a tech visionary and entrepreneur, is the CEO and Founder of Xeven Solutions, specializing in AI-driven healthcare, e-commerce, and digital marketing solutions. With over 10 years of experience, he has helped shape the tech landscape in Pakistan and abroad. Irfan has developed impactful AI applications and led international projects across the UK, US, and UAE. His achievements include being recognized as a top tech influencer.

SAQIB AZHAR

CEO ENABLERS

Saqib Azhar is a British-Pakistani entrepreneur and eCommerce mentor. He co-founded Enablers, an international platform that trains individuals and businesses in online selling, especially on Amazon. With over 15 years of experience in eCommerce, technology, and digital marketing, Saqib has guided many to start their own online businesses. He also created the world's largest Amazon eCommerce Facebook group, Ecommerce by Enablers. His efforts have earned him multiple awards, including four presidential awards in Pakistan.
